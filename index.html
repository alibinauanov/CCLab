Bacterimonia

Scientific Name:

Bacterimonia

Discovered by / at:

Alibi Nauanov, Spring 2025

Family:

A digital genus of interactive microorganisms, Bacterimonia mimics bacterial behavior in a simulated microscopic ecosystem. These digital bacteria move, adapt, and evolve in response to their environment.

Habitat:

A dynamic, fluid digital world that mirrors microscopic aquatic ecosystems, constantly shifting and reacting to stimuli.

Appearance:

Shimmering, shape-shifting bacteria with translucent membranes, flowing flagella, and bioluminescent hues that evolve as they consume particles.

Key Characteristics:

  • Moves organically in response to its environment.
  • Reacts dynamically to stimuli, including food particles.
  • Grows and changes shape based on interactions.
  • Exhibits swarm-like behaviors when multiple bacteria are present.

What we know so far about Bacterimonia? Bacterimonia is a real-time microbial simulation, visualizing unseen bacterial movement and interaction. Inspired by real bacteria, it transforms biological principles into an interactive digital experience, revealing the beauty of microbial life.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Move your cursor to guide the bacteria as they chase food and evolve. Their size, color, and behavior shift in response, offering an engaging glimpse into microscopic adaptation.
